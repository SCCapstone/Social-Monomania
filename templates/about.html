This webapp was created as a Computer Science Capstone project by several students at the University of South Carolina. This is meant to be a way to search multiple social media sites at one time and gain insight into trends shown on these sites. This webapp has very limited functions compared to full featured paid services.

Use the large search bar on the home page to begin a new search. Consult the frequently asked questions page for more help. If you run into a problem, feel free to use our contact us page to let us know about the issue.
