Q: What social media platforms does Social Monomania pull the results from?

A: Facebook,Instagram, Reddit, Twitter

Q: Can I save the results?

A: Yes, there is a downloadable excel file available at the end of every search

Q: What are the options for advanced search?

A: Geolocation, source such as Facebook only items, filter by time period

Q: Will any other social media platforms be added?

A: Yes, in the future we will be looking to implement more media platforms. Stay tuned.

Q: Why is there a login feature?

A: To prevent overloading our servers at the moment, we are restricting only authorized logins to use our features.

Q: How do I search within Facebook?

A: Make sure Facebook is selected for search results, then use hashtags (#) and keywords; or keywords by themselves.

Q: How do I search within Twitter?

A: Make sure Twitter is selected for search results, and use the following operators:

A: Operator: "happy hour" | Finds tweets: containing the exact phrase "happy hour."

A: Operator: love OR hate | Finds tweets: containing either "love" or "hate" (or both).

A: Operator: beer -root | Finds tweets: containing "beer" but not "root."

A: Operator: #haiku | Finds tweets: containing the hashtag "haiku."

A: Operator: near:NYC within:15mi | Finds tweets: sent within 15 miles of "NYC."

A: Operator: superhero since:2010-12-27 | Finds tweets: containing "superhero" and sent since date "2010-12-27" (year-month-day).

A: Operator: movie -scary :) | Finds tweets: containing "movie", but not "scary," and with a positive attitude.

A: Operator: flight :( | Finds tweets: containing "flight" and with a negative attitude.
